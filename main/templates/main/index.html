Trip Length Distribution

The service analyzes data on Trip Length Distribution (TLD) based on observed data, aiming to model the relationship between time and frequencies using a mathematical function tailored to the characteristics of trip length distribution.

Turning Movement Volumes

This service provides a programming solution to estimate the optimal Turning Movement Volumes (TMVs) at an intersection with an unlimited number of directions. The program analyzes the incoming and outgoing traffic flows from all possible directions, in addition to classifying the traffic movements for each direction based on their importance and impact.

" KMZ & KML File Processing Service!"

This service offers a powerful tool to upload and process KMZ and KML files, extracting geographic data and seamlessly converting it into Excel format. The areas of geographic shapes (Polygons) are calculated accurately, with names updated to include the area (in square meters), along with a hierarchical path reflecting the file’s structure. The modifications are saved in an updated KMZ file, ready for quick download. All of this with just a click, making working with geographic data easier and more organized!
